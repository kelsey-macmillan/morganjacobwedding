Guests will enjoy local food trucks, cigar bar, sparklers and champagne coupes. The groom’s family will be providing a whole pig and Vietnamese food for all to enjoy.

Children are welcome, but please note that our venue does not offer accommodations for naps, quiet or early bed times.
